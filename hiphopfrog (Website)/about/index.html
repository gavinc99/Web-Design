What is HipHopFrog?

HipHopFrog is a fully dedicated fan website on all things HipHop, we cover news, information and sell items on all of your favourite hiphop artists. It is our job to help you explore the worlds of both modern and classic hiphop genres.

How To Contact Us

You can contact us here or on any of our social media platforms: Facebook,Twitter or by email HipHopFrog@gmail.com

How Do I Find An Artist?

Go to the "Artists" tab on the navigation bar and it will list all of our current artists. Alternatively the "Home" page will show the most popular artists. If you have any artists you would like us to add please contact us here!

How Many Artists Are On HipHopFrog?

There are currently only 12 artists but we intend to add more, please give us your suggestions by contacting us here!
